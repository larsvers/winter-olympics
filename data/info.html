There are 5 interconnected parts here:
the story a map and three visuals.

The map is - well - a map and can be used as such. As an additional service it flies you to the destination you're reading about...

Each time you read about a certain event, the three visuals in the bottom change to display the respective data.
The titles are fairly self-explanatory...

The Nations and Medals visual shows each country as a square sorted by how many total medals each country got (not Gold only)

The Athletes and Gender visual shows each athlete as a circle distinguising between men in blue and women in orange. Hit the split button to split them out...

The Events visual shows each sport — of which there are surprisingly few, I find — in a treemap. Hovering over each sport shows the nested disciplines and the number of events per discipline...

Hit the Multiple button on each visual to see a chart for each Olympic event so far. The Athletes and Gender might take a moment to calculate — sorry — but is the most playful as you can split up and unite each athlete ball. Much fun!

If you want to browse yourself or just want to get to that one event hit that little triangle in the north-west...
